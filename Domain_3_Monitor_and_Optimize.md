# Domain 3: Monitor and Optimize an Analytics Solution

This domain covers 30-35% of the DP-700 exam. It focuses on ensuring your Fabric environment runs reliably, cost-effectively, and efficiently.

## 🗺️ Sub-Topics

1. [[09_Monitoring_and_Alerting]]
   - The Monitoring Hub.
   - The Capacity Metrics App.
   - Workspace-level Monitoring.
   - Configuring Alerts (Data Activator or Pipeline alerts).
   
2. [[10_Optimization_and_Troubleshooting]]
   - Identifying and resolving Pipeline and Dataflow errors.
   - Spark/Notebook Optimization (Partitioning, V-Order, Delta optimization).
   - SQL and KQL Query Optimization.

---
> *Tip: Monitoring is not just about errors; it's also about managing your Capacity Units (CUs). Know the difference between the Monitoring Hub (for job status) and the Capacity Metrics App (for billing and resource usage).*
